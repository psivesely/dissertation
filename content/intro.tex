\section*{Note on Authorship, Scope of Included Material, and Use of AI Tools}

Each chapter of this dissertation is adapted from joint work with co-authors, in which both the writing and the technical development were collaborative. In preparing this dissertation, I have therefore distinguished between expository material and core technical development. The core technical sections included here are limited to material of which I was the sole author or principal author, so that the dissertation's principal technical content reflects my primary research and writing contributions. By contrast, although I contributed to all of the introduction and technical overview sections included here, I was not the principal author of all of them. I have included this expository material to preserve the motivation, structure, and overall technical narrative of the underlying joint works, so that each chapter remains intelligible in context even where the detailed technical presentation is limited to portions that principally reflect my own authorship.

In preparing this manuscript, I used an AI-based tool (Claude) for limited purposes such as grammar review, editing assistance, and latex code-generation assistance. I bear full responsibility for the accuracy, originality, and proper attribution of all content in this dissertation.
